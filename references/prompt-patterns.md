# Prompt Patterns by Genre

Structured templates for common Veo 3.1 use cases.

## Nature & Wildlife

**Pattern:**
`[Shot] of [animal] [specific action] in [habitat], [time/weather], [camera move], [atmospheric detail], [style]`

**Example:**
> Wide shot of a red fox with white-tipped tail carefully stalking through snow-covered pine forest. Dawn blue hour, breath visible in freezing air. Slow tracking shot at eye level following from behind. Snowflakes falling gently. BBC Earth documentary style, 4K sharp detail.

**Keywords:** `migrating`, `pollinating`, `hunting`, `nesting`, `molting`, `stalking`, `pouncing`

## Product & Commercial

**Pattern:**
`[Shot type] of [product details] [action/demonstration] on [surface], [lighting setup], [camera movement], [audio cues], [mood]`

**Example:**
> Macro tracking shot of premium wireless earbuds rotating slowly on brushed black aluminum surface. Softbox lighting creating subtle reflection. 360° orbit with shallow depth of field. Sound: satisfying magnetic case snap, subtle electronic hum. Minimalist luxury aesthetic.

**Keywords:** `unboxing reveal`, `feature highlight`, `splash transition`, `sleek glide`, `satisfying click`

## Character & Narrative

**Pattern:**
`[Shot] of [character with distinctive details] [action/emotion] in [setting], [lighting], [camera], [audio dialogue], [style]`

**Example:**
> Medium shot of elderly woman wearing wire-rimmed glasses and indigo apron tending bonsai tree. Sunlit greenhouse, dust motes dancing in light beams. Slow dolly in, contemplative expression. Sound: soft pruning snips, peaceful sigh. Japanese wabi-sabi aesthetic, warm film tones.

**Consistency anchors:** Always include unique identifiers (glasses, scars, specific clothing items) for multi-clip consistency.

## Abstract & Experimental

**Pattern:**
`[Scale] of [material/substance] undergoing [transformation], [temporal quality], [lighting], [aesthetic]`

**Example:**
> Macro extreme close-up of ferrofluid responding to magnetic forces, forming intricate geometric spires. Slow-motion 240fps with hypnotic rhythm. Side lighting creating iridescent oil-slick colors. Minimalist experimental art aesthetic, clean black background.

**Physics to emphasize:** `fluid dynamics`, `magnetic fields`, `crystallization`, `combustion`, `acoustic vibrations`

## Architecture & Space

**Pattern:**
`[Shot] of [structure] [activity/emptiness], [time/weather], [camera movement], [scale reference], [atmosphere]`

**Example:**
> Extreme wide shot of empty brutalist concert hall with soaring concrete ceiling. Golden afternoon light streaming through geometric windows. Drone slowly rising to reveal full scale. Tiny human figure walking across stage for perspective. Contemplative, monumental, reverent silence.

## Action & Sports

**Pattern:**
`[Camera] following [subject] [action] with [physics detail], [speed], [environment interaction], [style]`

**Example:**
> Gimbal-stabilized tracking shot following skateboarder grinding concrete rail. Slow-motion 120fps capturing dust explosion and board flex. Urban environment with graffiti walls. Sound: wheels on concrete, scrape, landing thud. Energetic, street aesthetic, vibrant color.

## Food & Culinary

**Pattern:**
`[Shot] of [food action] showing [texture/physics], [lighting], [camera], [audio], [style]`

**Example:**
> Close-up of knife slicing through medium-rare steak, revealing pink interior. Steam rising, juices flowing. Side lighting emphasizing texture. Slow push-in. Sound: satisfying slice, sizzle, gentle plate contact. Food documentary style, appetizing warm tones.
