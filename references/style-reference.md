# Visual Style Reference

Veo 3.1 responds strongly to stylistic keywords. Use these modifiers to achieve specific aesthetic results.

## Film & Cinema

| Genre | Keywords | Mood |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Cinematic Standard** | `35mm anamorphic`, `Kodak Vision3`, `Panavision`, `film grain`, `cinematic color grade` | Professional, theatrical |
| **Noir** | `high contrast`, `chiaroscuro lighting`, `venetian blind shadows`, `foggy streets`, `low-key lighting` | Mysterious, dramatic |
| **Documentary** | `16mm handheld`, `natural lighting`, `verité`, `observational`, `available light` | Authentic, immediate |
| **Blockbuster/Epic** | `IMAX`, `epic scale`, `dramatic lighting`, `orchestral visual rhythm`, `sweeping vistas` | Grand, awe-inspiring |
| **Indie/Arthouse** | `shallow depth of field`, `naturalistic`, `muted tones`, `available light`, `intimate framing` | Personal, raw |

## Animation & Digital

## Lighting Conditions

**Time of Day**
- `Golden hour` — Warm, soft, just after sunrise/before sunset
- `Blue hour` — Cool, atmospheric, just before sunrise/after sunset
- `Magic hour` — Extended golden light
- `Harsh midday` — Hard shadows, high contrast, bright
- `Overcast` — Soft, diffused, even lighting

**Artificial Lighting**
- `Neon-noir` — Colored neon signs, wet streets, magenta/cyan
- `Practical lights` — Visible sources (lamps, screens, candles)
- `Cinematic three-point` — Key, fill, rim light setup
- `Volumetric god rays` — Light beams through atmosphere
- `Lens flare` — Anamorphic or spherical light artifacts

## Color Grading Palettes

| Grade | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `Teal and orange` | Blockbuster standard, skin tone pop, cyan shadows |
| `Bleach bypass` | Desaturated, high contrast, silvery, war-like |
| `Day for night` | Blue tint, underexposed, moonlit simulation |
| `Vintage Kodachrome` | Warm yellows, saturated reds, nostalgic |
| `Cyberpunk` | Magenta highlights, cyan shadows, neon accents |
| `Monochromatic` | Single color wash, sepia, cyanotype, black and white |
| `High saturation` | Vibrant, anime-like, commercial pop |
| `Muted/Earthy` | Desaturated, browns and greens, naturalistic |

## Texture & Quality Modifiers

- `Photorealistic` — Aim for reality
- `Hyperdetailed` — Maximum sharpness and micro-detail
- `Film grain 35mm` — Organic texture
- `Clean digital` — Sharp, noise-free, modern camera look
- `Shallow depth of field` — Bokeh, subject isolation
- `Deep focus` — Everything sharp, Gregg Toland style
- `Chromatic aberration` — Color fringing, lens imperfection
- `Lens breathing` — Subtle zoom shift during focus
